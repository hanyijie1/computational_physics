3.2 differential

3.2.1 Forward differential

$$ f'(x)=\frac{y_{n+1}-y_n}{h} $$

3.2.2 Backward differential

$$ f'(x)=\frac{y_{n}-y_{n-1}}{h} $$

3.2.3 Central differential

$$ f'(x)=\frac{y_{n+1}-y_{n-1}}{2h} $$
